Diabetic Retinophaty

Jul 1, 2011

Diabetic Retinopathy is the most common eye disease in American adults. The accelerated increase of the number of people with diabetes complications creates the need for systems that screen larger sections of the community effectively for early signs of diabetic retinopathy. This project was developed as an undergraduate student under supervision of prof. Jacques Wainer.
